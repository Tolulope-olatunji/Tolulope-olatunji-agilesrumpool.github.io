Our Policy

Company’s Policy At Toptopafric, we strive to provide exceptional service to all our customers, while maintaining a high level of safety and security. To ensure the smooth operation of our business and the satisfaction of our customers, we have developed the following policies:

Shipment Acceptance: We will only accept shipments that are properly packaged, labeled, and contain legal goods that are not prohibited by law or any government agency. We reserve the right to refuse any shipment that does not meet our criteria or poses a safety or security risk.

Shipment Insurance: We offer cargo insurance for our customer’s protection against the loss or damage of their goods during transit. However, customers are advised to review the terms and conditions of the insurance policy and determine if they need additional coverage.

Deliver Schedule: We will make every effort to deliver shipments on time as agreed upon with the customer. However, we cannot guarantee delivery times due to unforeseen circumstances such as weather conditions or road closures.

Payment Terms: Payment of our services is due upon delivery unless otherwise agreed upon in writing. We accept various forms of payments, including cash, check, and credit card.

Security Measures: We take security seriously and have implemented measures to ensure the safety of our shipments and drivers. This includes conducting background checks on all employees, using GPS tracking on our trucks, and implementing secure facilities for the storage and transfer of goods.

Customer Service: We value our customers and strive to provide exceptional service. If you have any questions or concerns about our policies and services, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are committed to resolving any issues promptly and to your satisfaction.

We appreciate your cooperation in adhering to our policies, which are designed to ensure a safe and efficient transportation experience.
